Photography

I believe that good photography shouldn't require good equipment. All too often, I feel that many people get too hung up on starting to take photos because they feel that their camera is inadequate. While I do typically shoot with a DSLR, my smartphone can often produce a quality image given the right lighting conditions. For professional tasks however, I will always use a DSLR because of their unparalleled speed, flexibility, and quality.
